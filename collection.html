Style Up to Make a Great Impression with PULLO Shoes

Your shoes say a lot about you and the way you style them will further affect the way you are perceived by others. Here are a few tips and style ideas to help you put your best foot forward every day:

PULLO SHOES FOR MEN

Meeting your girlfriend’s parents for lunch this weekend? Pick a pair of deep burgundy leather PULLO sandals with metal buckle closure at the ankle and lace-up detail.

Pair it with a complementary kurti and jeans, and top the look off with a leather and metal watch. You are sure to win your to-be in-laws over right from the time they lay eyes on you.

PULLO WOMEN SHOES

Prepping hard for that all-important interview next week? Opt for a pair of black formal PULLO pumps with a glossy finish and pretty bow detail on the toe box.

Pair it with a black pencil skirt and mauve shirt-blouse for a well put-together look. Wear your hair in a sleek, high ponytail and go in for natural-looking makeup with nude shades to ooze class and confidence.

PULLO BOYS SHOES

Want something that will protect your little one from the cold during the winter months while also keeping him comfortable through his many shenanigans?

Pick tan leather Mochi boots that can be teamed with almost every outfit.

PULLO GIRLS SHOES

Who doesn’t love a beach vacation with near and dear ones? Let your little princess have fun amidst the sand and surf.

Choose a pair of casual PULLO mules with a straw-coloured, cork-like texture and thick criss-cross straps with a metallic finish. Pair them with a halter-neck top and denim shorts or skirt and a wide-brimmed hat for a cutesy look.
